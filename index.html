About Us

We are dedicated to advocating for consumer rights and improving the health system in New Zealand. Our mission is to ensure that every consumer receives quality healthcare services and fair treatment.

Key Issues

  • Long wait times for essential services
  • Lack of access to specialized treatments
  • Inadequate communication between healthcare providers and patients

These issues highlight the urgent need for reform and improvement within the health system to better serve consumers.
